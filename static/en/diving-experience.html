What is Diving Experience?

Diving experience, also known as trial diving or discover diving, is an entry-level diving activity designed specifically for beginners. Without any diving experience or certification, you can safely explore the magical underwater world under the full accompaniment of a professional diving instructor.

Who is it suitable for?

  • Complete beginners who have never dived before and want to try it
  • Adventure lovers who are curious about the ocean world
  • People planning to learn diving and want to experience it first
  • Parents and children who want to experience family diving
  • Travelers who want to add a special experience to their trip

Experience Process

  • Safety Briefing: The instructor will introduce you to basic diving knowledge and safety precautions
  • Equipment Explanation: Learn how to correctly use diving equipment
  • Shallow Water Practice: Practice basic skills in shallow water (breathing, ear equalization, etc.)
  • Underwater Exploration: Dive into the water with the instructor to explore the beautiful underwater world
  • Share Experience: After diving, share your experience with the instructor and other participants

What You Will See

Depending on the diving location, you may see:

  • 🐠 Colorful tropical fish schools
  • 🪸 Spectacular coral reef ecosystems
  • 🐢 Leisurely swimming sea turtles
  • 🦈 Small sharks (depending on location)
  • 🪼 Various marine life and plants
