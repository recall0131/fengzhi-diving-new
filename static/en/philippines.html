🐙 Philippines

PHILIPPINES - Coral Reef Paradise

National Diving Resources

The Philippines consists of over 7,000 islands and is one of the world's top diving destinations. It's home to Tubbataha Reef, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, as well as world-famous dive sites like Bohol and Palawan.

Diving Features

  • Tubbataha Reef: UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Rich Coral Reefs: World-class coral reef ecosystems
  • Marine Biodiversity: One of the most biodiverse marine regions in the world
  • Barracuda Storms: Spectacular barracuda schools
  • Year-Round Diving: Water temperature remains 26-30°C year-round
  • Suitable for All Levels: From beginners to technical divers
