USA - Hawaiian Islands

National Diving Resources

Hawaii, USA is a world-famous diving destination with lava tube diving, rich coral reefs, and marine life like sea turtles and manta rays.

Diving Features

  • Lava Tube Diving: Unique lava tube dive sites
  • Coral Reefs: World-class coral reef ecosystems
  • Rich Marine Life: Sea turtles, manta rays, sharks, and more
  • Clear Waters: High visibility
  • Year-Round Diving: Water temperature 24-28°C
  • Suitable for All Levels: From beginners to technical divers
