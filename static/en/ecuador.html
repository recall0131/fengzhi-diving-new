🦎 Ecuador

ECUADOR - Galapagos Islands

National Diving Resources

Ecuador is home to the Galapagos Islands, a World Natural Heritage Site with unique marine ecosystems where you can see rare species like penguins, marine iguanas, and hammerhead sharks.

Diving Features

  • Galapagos Islands: World Natural Heritage Site
  • Unique Species: Penguins, marine iguanas, hammerhead sharks
  • Rich Marine Life: Manta rays, sea turtles, sharks, and more
  • Coral Reef Ecosystem: Healthy coral reef ecosystems
  • Year-Round Diving: Water temperature 23-27°C
  • Suitable for All Levels: From beginners to technical divers
