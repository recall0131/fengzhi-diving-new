🏝️ Maldives

MALDIVES - Tropical Paradise, Diving Destination

National Diving Resources

The Maldives consists of 26 atolls and approximately 1,200 coral islands, making it a world-class diving destination. Here you'll find crystal-clear waters, magnificent coral reefs, and abundant marine life.

Diving Features

  • Excellent Visibility:Water visibility often reaches 30-50 meters
  • Rich Coral Reefs:Healthy coral ecosystems with vibrant colors
  • Large Marine Life:Whale sharks, manta rays, sharks, and sea turtles are common
  • Atoll Dive Sites:Each atoll has unique dive sites
  • Year-Round Diving:Water temperature remains 26-30°C year-round
  • Suitable for All Levels:From beginners to technical divers
