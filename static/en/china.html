CHINA - Xisha Islands

National Diving Resources

The Xisha Islands in the South China Sea are China's top diving destination, with crystal-clear waters, rich coral reefs, and tropical fish schools.

Diving Features

  • Xisha Islands: Diving paradise in the South China Sea
  • Clear Waters: High visibility
  • Rich Coral Reefs: Healthy coral reef ecosystems
  • Tropical Fish Schools: Colorful tropical fish
  • Year-Round Diving: Water temperature 24-28°C
  • Suitable for All Levels: From beginners to technical divers
