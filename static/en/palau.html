🕳️ Palau

PALAU - World's Top Diving Destination

National Diving Resources

Palau is located in the Western Pacific and consists of over 200 volcanic and coral islands. It's one of the world's top diving destinations, famous for spectacular blue holes, jellyfish lakes, and abundant marine life.

Diving Features

  • Palau Blue Hole: World-famous blue hole dive site, spectacular geological wonder
  • Jellyfish Lake: Millions of harmless jellyfish, can be approached closely
  • Shark City: Swim with dozens of sharks
  • WWII Shipwrecks: Abundant wreck dive sites
  • Coral Reefs: Healthy coral ecosystems
  • Year-Round Diving: Water temperature remains 27-30°C year-round
