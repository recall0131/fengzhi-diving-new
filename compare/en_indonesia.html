🐠 Indonesia

INDONESIA - Land of Thousands of Islands, Diving Paradise

National Diving Resources

Indonesia consists of over 17,000 islands and is the world's largest archipelago nation. Here you'll find world-class diving destinations, including famous dive sites like Komodo, Bali, and Raja Ampat.

Diving Features

  • Komodo Dragons:The world's largest lizards
  • Manta Ray Aggregation:World's largest manta ray gathering site
  • Rich Coral Reefs:Healthy coral ecosystems
  • Biodiversity:One of the highest marine biodiversity regions in the world
  • Year-Round Diving:Water temperature remains 27-30°C year-round
  • Suitable for All Levels:From beginners to technical divers
