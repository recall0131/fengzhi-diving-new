🏛️ Egypt

EGYPT - Red Sea Diving Destination

National Diving Resources

Egypt's Red Sea is a world-famous diving destination with spectacular coral reefs, shipwrecks, and abundant marine life. It's a popular destination for European diving enthusiasts.

Diving Features

  • Coral Reefs: World-class coral reef ecosystems
  • Shipwrecks: Abundant wreck dive sites
  • Rich Marine Life: Sharks, manta rays, sea turtles, and more
  • Excellent Visibility: Crystal-clear waters with high visibility
  • Year-Round Diving: Water temperature 22-29°C
  • Suitable for All Levels: From beginners to technical divers
