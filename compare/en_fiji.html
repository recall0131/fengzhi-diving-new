🦈 Fiji

FIJI - Shark Paradise

National Diving Resources

Fiji is located in the South Pacific and consists of over 330 islands. It's a world-famous shark diving destination, known for shark feeding experiences where you can see up to 8 species of sharks.

Diving Features

  • Shark Feeding: See up to 8 species of sharks
  • Soft Corals: Soft coral capital of the world
  • Rich Marine Life: Manta rays, sea turtles, tropical fish
  • Atoll Dive Sites: Each atoll has unique dive sites
  • Year-Round Diving: Water temperature remains 26-29°C year-round
  • Suitable for All Levels: From beginners to technical divers
